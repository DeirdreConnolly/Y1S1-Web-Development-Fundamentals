About the Club

Welcome! We're just a bunch of students who discovered the fun team sport that is cycle ball! We hold regular training sessions for all levels of players, have a look at our weekly schedule below to see what goes on. All club members are welcome at our breakfast meetings, snacks and tea/coffee are provided. We also travel for matches at least one Saturday a month, keep an eye on the excursions timetable for information regarding the date/location. Have a look around, and if you're interested in joining our magnificent club, then sign-up! It's wheely fun. Club members have the option to purchase a t-shirt and/or hoodie to show off your club pride, ask within.
