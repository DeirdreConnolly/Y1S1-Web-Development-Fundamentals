Example of Special Characters (HTML Entities) in a Web Page

Seán and Sinéad are saving up for a trip abroad; they are planning on visiting Áine who is studying at the Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany. They plan on bringing a gift and decided that they will both pay ½ of the cost, however Mícheál asked if he could contribute towards the gift too and they will now pay 1/3 each, they have agreed on a total budget of €75 for the gift. Sinéad has suggested they try the new shop “Gifts & More” as there are great bargains advertised for this shop.

As they looked over the online catalogue for the shop, they came across the symbols for copyright ©, registered ® and trademark ™. These symbols were new to them and as they were studying HTML they decided to look up the codes for these so as to enter them on a web page.

Registered code = ®

Trademark code = ™
