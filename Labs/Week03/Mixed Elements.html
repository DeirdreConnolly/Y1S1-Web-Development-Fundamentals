Superscript & Subscript

These elements allow us to change content so that it displays above () or below () the normal line of text.

On the 4th of September you will learn about E=MC2.

The amount of CO2 in the atmosphere grew by 2ppm in 2009.

Quotations

Two elements are commonly used to marking up quotations: Blockquote and q. Blockquote is a block element, so it will position the quote on a new line q is an inline element, and it will enclose the content in " marks while remaining on the existing line of text.

Abbreviations & Acronyms

Both use the abbreviations element

Prof Stephen Hawking is a theoretical physicist and cosmologist.

NASA do some crazy space stuff.

Citations and Definitions

The cite element can be used when you are referencing a piece of work (e.g. book, research paper, film) to indicate where it is from.

The dfn element is used to indicate the defining instance of a new term.

A Brief History of Time by Stephen Hawking has sold over ten million copies worldwide.
A black hole is a region of space from which nothing, not even light, can escape.

Changes to Content: Inserting and Deleting Text

When content on a web page is being changed there are times when you want the original content to remain in place, but ensure that the visitor to your page knows it has been updated.
